1. Upload & Search

Get more information from your documents, faster.

DocumentCloud converts images and PDFs into searchable text, making it easy to browse through large sets of documents.

Use advanced search queries to quickly locate important keywords and add your own custom data to organize all your sources for a particular story.

2. Edit & Annotate

Highlight what matters and share your sources with colleagues.

Annotate key passages, pages, and sections in your documents.

Share documents, projects, and annotations to collaborate with journalists and experts inside and outside your newsroom.
